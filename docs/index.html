About Me

I'm a passionate professional with a background in Systems Analysis and ongoing studies in Architecture and Cloud. With 4+ years of experience in the technology field, I thrive as an SRE/DevOps specialist with deep expertise in infrastructure resilience and observability.

My focus lies in honing expertise in cutting-edge tools for infrastructure automation, containerization, and application monitoring. Proficient in automation languages such as Go, Node.js, and Python, I excel in optimizing processes and enhancing system performance and reliability.

I am the creator of the "Observability in Deep" course, where I share my knowledge on monitoring, tracing, and logging strategies for robust applications. Additionally, I hold the Certified Kubernetes Administrator (CKA) certification, demonstrating my expertise in managing and automating Kubernetes clusters.

I am driven by solving high-complexity problems and thrive in technical leadership roles. My ability to communicate effectively and work well with teams allows me to build strong, high-performing engineering organizations.
